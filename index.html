A little about us

Inu is a service that provides a public resource of dog friendly areas and increases social interactions among dogs and their owners. We know how important it is to include your furry friend in your daily activities, so our team is continuously working to update locations that are dog friendly.

Please be patient as our website is currently under construction and updates to release status can be found on our blog.

Overview of service:

Our initial release will feature dog-friendly open spaces, trails, restaurants, hotels, parks, and other public spaces in the Boulder, Colorado area. We hope to expand our services to include all of Colorado in the future.

For our next release, we will incorporate customizable user accounts that allow users to post pictures, rate locations, and comment on blog posts. Later updates will add new and improved features!

Brief Tutorial

For best results, allow our website to track your location. Your location is displayed by the icon.

  • Dog parks: These include both off and on-leash parks.
  • Hotels: These include hotels where dogs may stay with you in a room.
  • Stores: Businesses that will allow your dog to occumpany you inside.
  • Restaurants: These locations allow dogs on patios.
  • Veterinarian offices
  • Dog friendly trails: These include both on-leash and green tag areas.
